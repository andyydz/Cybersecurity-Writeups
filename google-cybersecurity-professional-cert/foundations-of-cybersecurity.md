Foundations of Cybersecurity

«Course 1 – Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate»

Overview

This document contains my personal notes and key takeaways from Course 1: Foundations of Cybersecurity in the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate.

The course introduces the fundamentals of cybersecurity, the responsibilities of security professionals, common cyber threats, security frameworks, ethics, and the core technical tools used by entry-level cybersecurity analysts.

«Note: These are my own learning notes and summaries, not copied course material.»

This course established the foundation for my cybersecurity journey.
